Case Study

Block by Block Tutoring — Brand & Website

Creating a friendly, trustworthy identity and digital presence for a tutoring service.

The challenge

Explain services clearly to parents and students while feeling approachable and fun.

Audience

Parents and students looking for math and general tutoring; local community.

My role & tools

Brand design, web design, content strategy; Tools: Canva, Figma, HTML/CSS.

Process

  • Explored color and shape language inspired by blocks (approachable, modular).
  • Designed logo, flyer, and IG post system with reusable components.
  • Built an accessible site structure with clear CTA flows.
  • User-tested messaging with a few prospective students/parents and iterated.

Outcomes

Consistent branding across print and web, with a website that makes it easy to contact and book sessions.
