UniFile — LegalTech Competition

AI-powered assistant concept for law firms.

Challenge

Law firms spend countless hours on research. We aimed to show how AI could streamline workflows responsibly.

Process

  • Brainstormed AI use cases (automation, chatbot intake, brief generator).
  • Mapped how UniFile would process documents and support lawyers.
  • Designed a polished poster to present the concept.

Outcome

A concept poster illustrating how firms could save time, improve accuracy, and modernize with AI.

Reflection

This project showed how UX design can translate complex tech into clear value. Next, I’d prototype and test with professionals.
